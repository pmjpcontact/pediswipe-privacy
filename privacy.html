Privacy Policy

PediSwipe — Pediatric Emergency Dosing for Apple Watch

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Summary

PediSwipe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

Data Collection

PediSwipe collects no data whatsoever. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies, and no third-party SDKs. No information leaves your device.

Data Storage

All app data (such as selected Broselow color zones) is stored locally on your Apple Watch and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

PediSwipe does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

PediSwipe is a clinical reference tool intended for healthcare professionals. It does not collect data from any users, including children.

Medical Disclaimer

PediSwipe is a clinical reference tool and does not replace professional medical judgment. All dosing information should be verified by the treating clinician. The developer assumes no liability for clinical decisions made using this app.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
